Statement.AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Statement.AI is built privacy-first. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your information. The same text is available inside the app under Settings → About → Privacy policy.

What we collect

Nothing, ourselves. Statement.AI has no account, no sign-in, and no first-party analytics. We operate no server that receives or stores your data. The third-party services described under Advertising and Subscriptions below operate under their own privacy policies.

Where your data lives

Everything you add — bank/card statements, transactions, balances, budgets, savings goals, PINs and statement passwords — is stored only on your device. Statement PDFs are unlocked and read on your phone. Categorization and all insights are computed on-device by Statement.AI's built-in engine. No external AI service or API is used.

What leaves your device

In normal use, your financial data never leaves your device. The only network request the core app makes is to load the PDF-reading library (pdf.js) when you open a statement; that request contains no personal or financial data.

Permissions we request

  • Files / document access — only to let you pick a statement PDF.
  • Notifications — only for reminders and budget alerts you turn on.
  • Face ID / biometrics — only to unlock the app, if you enable App Lock.
  • App Tracking Transparency — the free version asks your permission before any advertising tracking (see below). You can decline, and ads will be non-personalized.

Advertising (free version)

The free version may display ads via Google AdMob: a banner on the Dashboard and Insights screens, and optional "watch to unlock" rewarded ads. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier (IDFA / Advertising ID) and limited device/usage data, and may use it for tracking, under Google's privacy policy. On iOS this only occurs after you allow it via the App Tracking Transparency prompt. Premium removes all ads, and no ads are shown in the first 24 hours after install.

Subscriptions

Premium is an auto-renewable subscription processed by Apple (and managed by RevenueCat for entitlement validation). Payment is handled entirely by the App Store; we never see or store your card details. Apple and RevenueCat process purchase data under their own privacy policies.

Your controls

  • Delete everything any time: Settings → Delete all data.
  • App Lock with a 4-digit PIN and optional biometric unlock.
  • Decline ad tracking at the ATT prompt (or in iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking).
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data.

Children

Statement.AI is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date will change and the in-app copy will be revised.
